About me

I am someone who has been passionate about video games since my earliest memory in 2002 when I used to play on the Nintendo 64 at just 4 years old. Video games have remained my greatest passion and I have never stopped playing them. In high school, I discovered that I had a gift for understanding mathematical concepts and solving problems, which led to it becoming my favorite subject alongside natural science. At University, I pursued a degree in Physics and Mathematics, but I eventually realized that my true passion lay in writing code. This discovery reignited my love for gaming and motivated me to make a change. So, I made the bold decision to leave University earlier than planned and enroll in Algonquin College to pursue a career in software engineering. My ultimate goal is to design and code my own video games, something that combines my passion for gaming and my love of coding.
